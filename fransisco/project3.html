Sub Steps For a Sustainable Process

Webplatform Layout Motion Graphics Art Direction

2020

A reflection on design methods, an introspection into the current process in the design industry, and an urge to improve the way we work into a more sustainable and community-based design. Layout for a website, that intends to bring the design community together to introduce more sustainable techniques in their process. A virtual platform where practitioners can get specific advice on how to take the extra step to practice a more sustainable graphic design. To enhance the sense of community, you can share your own and check more tips suggested by other practitioners.
